About Me

Who I Am

Hey there! I’m Jahnavi Shah, a first-year Computer Engineering student with a curious mind, a creative soul, and an ever-growing passion for building meaningful digital experiences. Whether it’s brainstorming startup ideas with friends, designing user-friendly interfaces, or diving into hackathons, I love exploring the world of tech from both the logical and artistic lens.

I’ve participated in a few hackathons, where I got hands-on experience with problem-solving, team collaboration, and turning ideas into working prototypes under tight deadlines. These experiences have taught me the thrill of innovation and the importance of adaptability—qualities I carry into every project I take on.

Outside the world of code, I hold a master’s degree in Bharatanatyam, which keeps me rooted in discipline, storytelling, and culture. I also enjoy traveling and landscape photography, constantly seeking new perspectives and inspiration from the world around me.
